26th Feb 2018

Rock On!! - Farhan Akhtar (Rock On!!, 2012)

One of my favourite musical movies. Great soundtracks. "Pichle saath dino me" is my personal favourite but this is the title track.

Don't Stay in School - Boy in a Band (2015)

Song about the flaws of the educational system. The "Don't stay in school" is for the useless lessons and not the students.

Small Town Girl - Shankar Mahadevan (Bachna Ae Haseeno, 2008)

You don't get more "Classic Bollywood than this. The MC's name is literally Raj.

Beauty of Annihilation - Elena Siegman (Call of Duty, 2011)

Easter egg song from the Call of duty Zombies series. Most of these are Metal. I suggest checking out 115, Pareidolia and Abracadavre

I want money - Govinda (Wah Tera kya kehena, 2002)

Good money, Bad money, Wrong money, Right money, Black money, White money, Oh my gooooooodddddd.....
